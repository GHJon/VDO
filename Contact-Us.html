Hotlines

National Domestic Violence Hotline

The National Domestic Violence Hotline is dedicated to help the lives of thousands of victims, survivors, and their families to get the care they need. They are available 24/7 to talk confidentially with anyone experiencing Domestic Violence, seeking resources or information, or questioning their relationship.

Phone: 1-800-799-SAFE

Website: http://www.thehotline.org

New York Police Department (911 Operator)

In an emergency, call 911 immediately from any kind of phone. It is essential if you're being abused or attacked in any way that means harm.

Website: http://www.911.gov

Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline

The Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline is dedicated to the prevention of child abuse. They are available 24/7 with professionnal counselers confidentially, in which overall, we can assist with languages other than English. They offer crisis prevention, information, and referrals towards others that can assist you.

Phone: 1-800-4-A-CHILD

Website: https://www.childhelp.org

National Sexual Assault Hotline

The National Sexual Assault Hotline provides confidential, judgement-free support from a trained staff member. They also provide with local resources that can assist you with your next steps towards healing and recovery. You may also receive support via a confidential online chat via https://ohl.rainn.org/online

Website: https://www.rainn.org

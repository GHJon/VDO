Who Are We?

Founded in 1994, The Violence Defense Organization is a New York only organization that aims to help those who are victims of any type of violence, whether it be physical, verbal, or on the internet. We provide information for all aspects of violence and how to respond to those abusive actions. Through our website, we will give information for those that don't know precisely what Violence is, how to respond to it, our office hours, contact page, hotlines for victims and more in order to make Violence drastically decrease in our community. With our committed staff, we will keep this website up and running for anybody who wishes to understand the issue of Violence. Due to our existance, we have lowered gun violence drastically over the past 20 years, ans we intend to keep it that way as well as lower violence in all other aspects of it. We must protect those that are taken advantage of through every single outlet.
